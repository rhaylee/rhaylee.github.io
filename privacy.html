Privacy Policy for Arcane Focus

Last Updated: March 2026

Rhaylee Dreams - Shop of Wonders ("we", "our") operates the Arcane Focus mobile application.

1. Data Collection

Arcane Focus does not collect or store any personal data. Your task lists, mana levels, and NPC data are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to our servers.

2. Advertising (AdMob)

We use Google AdMob to serve advertisements. AdMob may collect and use anonymous data (such as device identifiers and location) to show you relevant ads.

3. Children's Privacy

Our app does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal identifiable information from children.

4. Contact

If you have questions, contact us via the Shop of Wonders support channels.
